HOW IT ALL STARTED


It all began when Hyomin and Taewook crossed paths through their mutual friend, Seula Naomi, in February 2021. In need of assistance for her work, Hyomin reached out to Naomi, who promptly suggested Taewook as the perfect solution. Intrigued, Hyomin started following Taewook but didn't initiate any conversation. Fortunately, Taewook, recognizing Hyomin's name from his connection with another mutual friend, mustered the courage to say hello.

Their friendship blossomed quickly thereafter. Before Taewook departed for the United States, he expressed his desire for a long-distance relationship and made a heartfelt promise to visit Hyomin at least once every three months. True to his word, the couple established a routine of traveling between cities in the United States and Korea, creating cherished moments together.

As the world grappled with the COVID-19 pandemic, they found themselves spending more time together than ever before, and amidst this closeness, a beautiful and meaningful relationship took root. Their aspirations, dreams, hopes, and future plans perfectly aligned, paving the way for a remarkable journey together.

Now, with hearts filled with joy and gratitude, Hyomin and Taewook warmly invite you to witness and celebrate their union as they joyfully exchange vows and say "I do!" Their love and gratitude extend to their beloved families and cherished friends, whose unwavering presence and encouragement have been instrumental in shaping their journey towards this significant milestone.
